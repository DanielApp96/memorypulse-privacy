🧠 MemoryPulse — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

MemoryPulse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application MemoryPulse.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Usage data: Game scores, levels completed, and best scores stored locally on your device.
  • Advertising data: Our app uses Google AdMob to show advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized ads.
  • Purchase data: If you subscribe to MemoryPulse Pro, your purchase is processed through Google Play. We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect your Google Play purchase token and device identifiers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve the app experience
  • To show relevant advertisements (free users)
  • To process and manage subscriptions
  • To track best scores locally on your device

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — for subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Google Play — for app distribution and in-app purchases. Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage

Your game scores and preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not store this data on external servers.

5. Children's Privacy

MemoryPulse is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising (via your device settings)

7. Advertising Opt-Out

You can opt out of personalized ads by going to your device Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
